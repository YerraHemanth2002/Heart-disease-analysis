Heart Disease Analysis

Heart disease analysis typically refers to the process of examining various factors related to heart health to assess the risk, presence, or severity of heart disease. This can include analyzing medical history, symptoms, diagnostic test results (such as electrocardiograms or blood tests), lifestyle factors (like diet and exercise), and sometimes genetic predispositions. The goal is to provide insights into the condition of the heart and develop appropriate treatment plans or preventive measures.
